🧬 STUDY: 4.3 UNIT TEST - EVOLUTION
What is Evolution?
Evolution is the change in traits of a population over time.
Who is Charles Darwin?
Scientist who developed the theory of natural selection after studying the Galápagos Islands.
What is Natural Selection?
Organisms with better traits survive and reproduce more successfully.
Evidence of Evolution?
Fossils, DNA, embryology, and homologous structures.
Homologous vs Analogous?
Homologous = same structure, different function.
Analogous = same function, different structure.
